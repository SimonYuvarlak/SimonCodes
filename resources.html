Pexels

One of the hardest job as a designer is to find good quality pictures that you can use. I frustrated a lot till I found Pexels. Literally it is my favorite web site. You can download good quality pictures for free and you have variety of pictures.

What makes it special is that you can have a profile like a social platform, have your own collections, can see liked pictures and so on. And last but not least my favorite functionality, you can download full picture or specify the size you need. That way you do not have to work on the picture after you download it.

I recommend it to designers, developers, students, business men, nurse, cook, does not matter what you do. You do not need to be a developer or designer to enjoy Pexels. It is basically a good collection of quality photographs.

Check out Pexels

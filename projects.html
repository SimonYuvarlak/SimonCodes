Votebox

Simple voting dApp for learning purposes (currently ongoing). Votebox dApp is implemented with Rust and cosmwasm library, vote related data is stored on Juno testnet. Client side is implemented with CosmJS and MaterialUI, in order to execute any functions from the smart contract, user must have a Keplr wallet and JUNOX coin. Querying other voteboxes from the blockchain is free and do not require Uni-2 network connection.

Check Out Votebox
